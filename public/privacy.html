Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Clear Woods Crew ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website clearwoodsnc.com. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our site or submit a quote request.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out our quote request form, including:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Service type requested
  • Property details you choose to share

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, through standard web analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your quote request and contact you about our services
  • Schedule estimates and follow up on inquiries
  • Improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use our site. We use Google Analytics and Google Ads tracking to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by visiting tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

4. Third-Party Services

Our quote form is powered by GoHighLevel (GHL), a CRM platform. Information submitted through our form is stored and processed by GoHighLevel in accordance with their privacy policy. We use GHL to manage leads and customer communications only.

We use Google Ads and may use Google Analytics to measure website traffic and advertising performance. These services may collect information about your visits to our site using cookies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact information as long as necessary to provide our services and maintain our business records. You may contact us at any time to request deletion of your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
