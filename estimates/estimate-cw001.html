Scope of Work

Forestry mulching and brush clearing for approximately 2 acres along the right fence line, beginning from the front portion of the property and clearing through to the rear property corner, as discussed during the site visit.

Work will include:

  • Grinding and mulching brush, saplings, and small trees
  • Vegetation reduction to ground level
  • Creation of a natural mulch layer from processed vegetation
  • Clearing access through the designated area

Mulching leaves behind organic ground cover, helping reduce erosion and future regrowth.
